About & Resume

My name is Shaam Adam and I am a GIS Specialist with U-Spatial at the University of Minnesota. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Geography with a minor in Geographic Information Science in 2023. I am currently working on my Masters of Geographic Information Science with a minor in Systems & Industrial Engineering. I am skilled in mapping, web GIS, python (arcpy, geopandas, pandas) and visualizations. Experienced in data analysis, automating geoprocessing workflows and building interactive web applications for decision-making.

  • ArcGIS Pro / ArcGIS Online / Experience Builder
  • Python (ArcPy, pandas), automation
  • Raster & vector analysis, tile caching, publishing
  • Cartography, UX for map apps
